Our Story

In 2009 Doggster was founded by Nicole McCarthy. Driven by a passion for dogs, Nicole began the training school on her own. It started as a small scale business offering basic dog training with an emphasis on dog behaviour modification. Nicole had a strong belief that every dog could be trained and that it's up to us to understand their needs which is something we still go by today.

Over 10 years later we can say that there's not a single challange we haven't overcome. Having trained over 3500 dogs up to date we make sure to go above and beyond with our training and combine our knowledge and skills to provide the best training there is!

“Dogs do speak, but only to those who know how to listen.”

Our Mission

Our aim at Doggster is to help owners build a relationship with their dogs based on trust and respect. We are here to give you the skills and understanding of how dogs learn and what motiveates their behavoiur, it's up to the owner to keep praticing and evolving with their pooches.

We go by "Learning by doing" philoshopy which requires a lot of patience and effort but at the end the whole training process is a gratification for both owner and the dog.

"Dog training is a never-ending process of evolving."
