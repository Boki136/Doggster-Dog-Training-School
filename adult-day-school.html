Day School

We know how hard it is to leave your best friend at home while you're in work. Our Day School programme is the best place for your furry friend to spend the energy and socialize with others. During the each sessions we also cover basic training to ensure your dog is making progress as well.

Have any quesitons or concers feel free to reach out to us!

  • Session duration is 4hrs
  • MONDAY - FRIDAY, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Drop off between 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM, pick up between 12:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • With a 6 dog maximum, your dog will always receive personalized attention
  • Exclusively for dogs older than 5 months. Have a pup? Check out our Puppy Day School
  • This programme is only for dogs with friendly behaviour. All dogs over 7 months must be neutered / spayed to attend the class
